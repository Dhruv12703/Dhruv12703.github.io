BMW Sales Performance Dashboard (2010–2024)

Tools: Power BI

Overview

Built an interactive dashboard to analyze BMW sales performance by year, region, and model. The report highlights revenue trends, top-selling models, and pricing insights.

Key Metrics

  • Total Revenue (Price_USD × Sales_Volume)
  • Total Sales Volume
  • Average Price
  • Average Mileage
